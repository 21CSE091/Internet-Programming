BUS

    FEATURES:

  1. A bus is a vehicles having at least four wheels, designed and constructed for the carriage of passengers, comprising more than eight seats in addition to the driver’s seat.
  2. Buses improve social inclusion, providing access to education, employment & healthcare to all–including those on low incomes, those who do not drive, the older generation, people with disabilities and people living in remote areas.
  3. Buses are the most widely-used form of public transport in the world, serving cities as well as suburban and rural areas.
  4. They are also the most cost-efficient and flexible form of public transport, requiring minimal investments to launch new lines or routes.
  5. Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) systems have gained popularity worldwide as a cost-effective alternative to far more expensive urban rail investments.
  6. With one bus capable of replacing 30 cars on the road, buses help ease traffic congestion.
  7. Buses have the lowest carbon footprint per passenger of any form of motorised transport.
  8. Buses are a safe transport mode, responsible for just 2% of road fatalities in the world.
  9. Double decker buses have a greater number of seats for a given overall length than a single Decker but stability is not so high as for the single Decker
  10. Buses give out 20% lesser carbon monoxide, 75% of nitrogen oxides, and 10% lesser hydrocarbons per passenger kilometer than a vehicle with a single driver.
