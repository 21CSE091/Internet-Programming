The city Madurai is the second largest city in Tamil Nadu, is known for its world renowned Meenakshi Temple. This Meenakshi temple is famous for its intricate carvings and scriptures. The city of Madurai is situated at the bank of river Vaigai and boasts of a rich ancient culture and heritage. Madurai is more than 2,500 years old; it is one of the most ancient cities of India.
